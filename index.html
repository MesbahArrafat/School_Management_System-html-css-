About Us

Rights for Special Education.

The need for opening an English Medium School was first spelt out by the then Area Commander of Mymensingh Area, Major General M Harun-Ar-Rashid, BP, rcds, psc. It became a fact when MIS started its journeywith the classes from Pre-Nursery to Std-II, on 19 January 2000 with only 36 students, 06 teachers and few number of staff.

Our mission is to create lifelong learners who will aspire to build a better world.

Our aim is instilling in them critical thinking skills, a global perspective and a respect for core values of honesty, loyalty, perseverance and compassion.

Mymensingh International School has started to walk through the way to work on their dream to create a vibrant institution that provides highest standard of education for students to develop them tint ethically strong and morally elevated individuals.
